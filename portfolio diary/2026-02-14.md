## Thought Process and Tradeoffs
- Chose remote state early because it is foundational for team-safe IaC workflows and interview credibility.
- Kept design single-region (`us-east-1`) with production-style intent, to show realistic architecture while controlling complexity.
- Retained a clear naming convention (`chesschat-<env>-<service>-<purpose>`) to keep resources easy to reason about during interviews.
- Preferred pragmatic defaults over advanced multi-account/multi-region setup at this stage to keep momentum.
