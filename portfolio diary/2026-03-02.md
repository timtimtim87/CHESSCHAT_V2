### Why These Decisions
- Queue-based finalization decouples user-visible game completion from persistence reliability and prevents state divergence on transient DynamoDB failures.
- Idempotent DynamoDB transaction semantics ensure safe retry/replay behavior without duplicate stat updates.
- Reconnect versioning plus stale-deadline cleanup creates monotonic, interview-defensible lifecycle semantics under disconnect/reconnect races.
- Lifecycle transition logs make it easier to correlate user reports with server-side transitions during incident triage.
